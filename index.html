The 30-Day TRUTH Process

From Trust-Based Authority to a Verified, Machine-Readable Model

We use a structured, time-boxed process called TRUTH to baseline your authority model. It’s designed to be executive-friendly but technically precise enough to plug into your identity, contracts, and AI roadmap.

You bring your current structures (org charts, delegations of authority, board minutes, trust documents, etc.). We bring the TRUTH framework and Kontrak IQ’s authority mapping lens.
TRUTH Framework
Trace → Reveal → Unify → Test → Harden.

A pragmatic, five-step sequence to move authority from “we think” to “we know” in a way your AI, contracts, and controls can actually use.

T1 — Trace: Where Does Authority Live Today?
Days 1–5

We inventory how authority is currently defined and exercised across key domains: legal, finance, procurement, operations, and technology.

Inputs: org charts, DOA PDFs, board minutes, trust docs, signatory lists, contract playbooks, system roles.
R — Reveal: Where Are the Gaps, Conflicts, and Risks?
Days 5–10

We surface misalignments, conflicts, and blind spots: people with power but no record, records with power no one uses, and workflows that ignore the authority rules on paper.

Output: heatmap of high-risk authority areas across entities, processes, and systems.
U — Unify: A Single, Coherent Authority View
Days 10–18

We consolidate your authority picture into a single logical model: roles, limits, conditions, and exceptions that can be read by both humans and machines.

Output: draft digital authority map (who can do what, where, and under which conditions).
T2 — Test: Run Real-World Scenarios
Days 18–24

We pressure-test the model against real scenarios: contract approvals, wire releases, major purchases, write-offs, AI agent actions, and trustee decisions.

Output: list of authority rules that hold, fail, or need refinement before automation or AI.
H — Harden: Board-Ready & AI-Ready Baseline
Days 24–30

We finalize a board-ready, machine-readable authority baseline with clear next-step options for identity, contracts, AI, and controls.

Output: Digital Authority Baseline™ report and implementation playbook.
